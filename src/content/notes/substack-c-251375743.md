The interesting fact is that good _enough_. 

4.6 is good, 4.7 is better but also more expensive and slower, so engineers vote with their feet. They stay with 4.6. 

The interesting insight is that, a capable enough model is just enough. Soon open models will catch up, inferences will compete. Coding just need enough intelligence. 

Business moat will stand long, not due to intelligence, but due to domain insights.
