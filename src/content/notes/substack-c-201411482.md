Work hard, work long, and work smart, you cannot succeed with 2 out of 3. This is a popular quote from JeffB. At first I thought I need to do 2 of them (allow my poor English); then I know it’s actually all 3; now I say, actually need 4th factor: luck (sometimes people call it timing).
