Addictive self destruction. 

Just can’t stop building fully autonomous agent. 

I told my manager, I hope this can replace myself in six months. 

You hold an edge with hard work, but soon everyone catch up, or being forced to, with new model versions. 

It’s easy to execute, but it’s hard to think for longer than three months.
