I heard the podcast, the conviction was not there.

Someone came to a design review and stated that the system will be 100x faster, which is ambitious and exciting, then I’d ask technical details how they achieve that. They cannot just argue that “trust me bro“, or “if you don’t sign-off this design I don’t need you to be reviewer, and of course you will no be on my launch thanks list“.

My engineering integrity will just push myself away from such design review.
