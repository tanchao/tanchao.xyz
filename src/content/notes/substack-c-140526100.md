The “trust” problem. 

The trade off between _restrictions_ and _user__experiences_; and the balance among consumers, content creators, tool(model) providers, platform owners; are the product challenges. 

The realtime check and keep it up to date with an async algorithm/model refresh, the observability and auditing of an judgement, the life circle management of an event, on top of a high volume low latency distributed system; are the technical challenges. 

There are lots of fun.
