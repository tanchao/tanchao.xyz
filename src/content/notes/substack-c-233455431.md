The tech world will become silo. 

It’s so cheap to personalize tool chain and customize my own workflow. I can easily refer others best practices (as long as they share publicly) and merge into mine. 

Those are treasures for the future. I’m silo and will not open. Just that’s the way to keep an edge in the new world.
